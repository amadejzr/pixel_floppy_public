## Before You Write
Pixel Floppy is fully offline.  
This means:

- I **cannot access your data**  
- I **cannot recover deleted data**  
- I can only see your logs if you toggeled Crash Reporting.

So when reporting a problem, the more details you provide, the faster and more accurate the help will be.

---

## How to Report a Bug

When reporting an issue, please include:

1. **Short description**  
   What happened? What did you expect to happen?

2. **Steps to reproduce**  
   A simple list of how you got to the issue, e.g.:  
   1. Open the app  
   2. Create a tracker  
   3. Tap “Log entry”  
   4. Crash appears  

3. **Device information**  
   - iPhone model  
   - iOS version  
   - Pixel Floppy version

## Privacy

Your data stays **only on your device**.  
No accounts, no cloud sync, and no remote access only for the crash and logs if you enable it.

Support never sees your data unless you explicitly choose to share a crash report.
